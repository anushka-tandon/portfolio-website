Welcome to my world!
“Everything is a story- you are a story – I’m a story. For this world is not made of atoms. It is a conglomerate of our life’s tales. One big, giant narrative.”
Stories have always played an important role in my life. Coming from a place, where the art of story-telling has always existed since thousands of years, I always wanted myself to be a part of such a legacy. Where I could narrate my own take, my interpretation of this world. My story in my voice.
Modern School
NIFT
FIT
I graduated from the prestigious Modern School, New Delhi India, where I was exposed to diverse visual mediums and arts which excited me towards the field of Design. I gained admission to the coveted National Institute of Fashion Technology, New Delhi, where I studied “Fashion Communication”. The course made me hone my skills on different mediums of communication such as graphic design, photography, visual merchandising and styling.


To excel in the field, I studied “Communication Design” at FIT, New York where my creativity was exposed to advertising design, web design, visual presentation and exhibition design quenching my understanding on the nuances of practical commercial communications. I firmly believe designs have to be technologically aesthetic and pragmatic.
MY BELIEFS
Never give up
I strive for perfection, and I refuse to give up. When deadlines are looming and pressure is building, I make sure that I face the challenge head on and complete with perfection, always. As is known, pressure builds diamonds.
I feel you
My work reflects the emotional quotient and deep essence. I do not believe in working for the sake of it, but the value it contributes overall.
Come upfront
My work looks simple at a glance, but deep within there are many layers embedded, there’s a story behind everything which add an element of surprise and a deep meaning to my final work.
